<Info>
  Feature available on the Growth plan
</Info>

Apple requires you to enable access to Appstore Connect API. To enable this access you need to be an Account Holder and follow this steps on [Appstore Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/login).

1. On Users and Access page select the integrations page.
2. Click on Request access.
3. Agree to the terms and press Submit.

This needs to be completed before you can start the process to connect Appstore Connect with Appinion.
