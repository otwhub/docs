### What you’ll achieve

In less than 10 minutes, you will:

- Connect your app to Appinion
- Import user reviews from the app stores
- Start exploring topics, sentiment, and summaries

### Before you start

- An Appinion account
- Access to App Store Connect and or Google Play Console
- At least one published app

That’s it. No SDKs or code required.

### Create your account

If you haven’t already:

1. Sign up for an Appinion account
2. Log in to the dashboard

Once logged in, you’re ready to connect your first app.
